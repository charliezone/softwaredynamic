Possible reasons for under-conversion:
- the price seems too low, which may reduce trust
- some visitors are curious, not ready to buy
- WhatsApp click is still a high-intent action for cold traffic

- the page may still need better message match with the ads
- the page may need stronger social proof above the fold
- the CTA wording may need to feel lower commitment

Important: this is a local service business funnel, so users may need more reassurance than impulse e-commerce traffic.
